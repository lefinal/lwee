lwee [arguments] [command] [context directory]

Commands:
    create-action   Creates the specified action.
    init            Initializes a new project in the context directory.
    verify          Verifyies the project with the flow file in the context directory.

Examples:
    lwee init .
    lwee init /tmp/my-project
    lwee verify .
    lwee verify -f other-flow.yaml .
